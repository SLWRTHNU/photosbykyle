How It Works

The Process

From First Contact to Final Image

Every detail, considered.

A process built around preparation, transparency, and craft — so you can focus on your business while I focus on making imagery that honours the same care and attention to detail you put into yours.

01

Step One

Before a single light is set,
we talk.

A 30-minute conversation that costs nothing and shapes everything.

I want to understand your brand the way you do — the standards you hold yourself to, the story behind what you're building, and what success actually looks like for this project. From there, I'll send a detailed proposal with no strings attached.

Free 30 minutes No obligation Detailed proposal to follow
02

Step Two

The shot exists
before the shoot does.

Once we've confirmed the project, the real work begins.

I develop a complete visual plan — shot list, mood board, prop and styling direction, and full logistics for the day. You'll know exactly what to expect before a single piece of equipment leaves the bag. No surprises. Just great preparation.

Shot list Mood board Prop guidance Location scouting if needed
03

Step Three

Focused. Precise.
Genuinely enjoyable.

Shoot day is calm, professional, and built around bringing out the best in your food, your space, and your people.

We move through the plan with intention, staying open to the moments that go beyond it. Live previews keep you in the loop throughout. You're welcome on set — most clients say it's one of their favourite parts of the whole process.

Professional lighting On-set art direction Live previews You're welcome on set
04

Step Four

Where good
becomes unforgettable.

Every image is colour-graded, retouched, and export-ready before it ever reaches you.

Delivered through a private online gallery, formatted for web, social, and print — within the agreed timeline. Every image comes with full lifetime commercial usage rights, so you own them outright and can use them however and whenever you need, with no expiry and no hidden licensing fees.

Full retouch High-res & web formats Private gallery delivery 7–10 business days Commercial rights included
