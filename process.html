How It Works

The Process

From First Contact to Final Image

A seamless experience from start to finish

I've designed every step of the process to be clear, collaborative, and stress-free — so you can focus on your business while I focus on creating imagery that elevates it.

01

Discovery Call

Before anything else, we talk. I want to understand your business, your goals, and what success looks like for this project. This call is free, relaxed, and helps us both make sure we're the right fit.

We'll discuss your brand story, the platforms you're creating content for, and any visual references or inspiration you have in mind.

Free 30-minute call — no obligation
Talk through your brand, goals, and timeline
I'll send a detailed proposal and quote after
02

Creative Planning

Once we've confirmed the project, I develop a full shot plan — including a detailed shot list, mood board, prop and styling direction, and logistics for the shoot day.

You'll know exactly what to expect before we ever pick up a camera. No surprises, just great preparation.

Detailed shot list tailored to your goals
Mood board & visual direction for approval
Prop sourcing and food styling guidance
Location scouting if needed
03

The Shoot

Shoot day is focused, efficient, and — dare I say — enjoyable. I create a calm, professional environment so your team, food, or products can look their best.

We work through the shot list with intention, leaving room to improvise and capture moments that go beyond the plan.

Professional lighting and equipment
On-set styling and art direction included
Collaborative — you're welcome to be present
Previews shared on the day for peace of mind
04

Editing & Delivery

After the shoot, I carefully select and retouch the best images from the session. Every delivered file is colour-graded, retouched, and export-ready for your intended use.

Images are delivered via a private online gallery within the agreed timeline, formatted for web, print, and social media use.

Professional retouching on all delivered images
High-resolution files for print and web
Private online gallery delivery
Commercial usage rights included
Typical turnaround: 7–10 business days
