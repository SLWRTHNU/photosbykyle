About Kyle

The eye
behind the lens

I'm Kyle Beaulieu. I grew up around cameras, tagging along on shoots with my father, and that early exposure never really left me. What pulled me toward food and branding photography specifically was the restaurant world. The chaos, the elegance, and the extraordinary attention to detail that goes into crafting a dish is like nothing else. The colors, the textures, the placement of every element on a plate. I've worked in kitchens, I love to cook, and I genuinely admire the craft that goes into what I photograph.

That admiration shows up in my work. If something is visually off, even by a degree, or there's a crumb somewhere it shouldn't be, I can't let it go until it's right. I adapt quickly, stay calm under pressure, and bring that same obsessive eye to every single shoot.

Photography is also how I give back. Any organization, event, or cause connected to Type 1 Diabetes receives complimentary photography, no strings attached. T1D hits close to home, and if my work can support that community in any way, I'm honoured to do it. If that's you, please reach out.
